Privacy Policy for Shekel App

Effective Date: 06-Oct-2023

Introduction

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") is designed to help you understand how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use our mobile application, Shekel, and the services provided within the app.

Information We Collect

We may collect and store the following types of personal information:

  1. User Profile Information: When you create an account with Shekel, we may collect information such as your name, email address, and profile picture.
  2. Financial Data: Shekel allows you to manage financial transactions between parents and children. We may collect and store transaction data, including amounts, dates, and descriptions.
  3. Device Information: We may collect device-specific information, such as the type of device you use, unique device identifiers, and operating system version.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for various purposes, including:

  1. Providing Services: To provide the core functionality of Shekel, including facilitating money management between parents and children.
  2. Account Management: To manage user accounts, verify identities, and reset passwords when necessary.
  3. Customer Support: To respond to user inquiries, requests, and support needs.
  4. Analytics: To analyze user interactions with the app, improve user experience, and enhance app performance.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:

  1. With Your Consent: We may share your information with third parties if you give us your consent.
  2. For Legal Reasons: We may share information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid court order.

Security

We are committed to protecting your personal information and have implemented reasonable security measures to prevent unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

Your Choices

You may have choices regarding the collection and use of your personal information. You can:

  • Access, correct, or delete your information.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.
  • Close your Shekel account.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at contact@asafdavid.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Please review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
